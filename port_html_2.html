Orange Panda Poster Series

Objective

Design six immersive 3D visuals for "Orange Panda" to showcase its innovative audio products, targeting young tech enthusiasts.

Approach

  • Inspiration: Futuristic 3D designs, minimalist layouts, and thin typography.
  • Color Palette: Black for sophistication, orange for vibrancy.
  • Typography: Clean bunya fonts for modern appeal.
  • Imagery: 3D product models emphasizing the premium quality of the audio products.

Tools

cinema4D for 3D renders and design execution.

Outcome

A cohesive and visually striking poster series that effectively conveys the brand's futuristic identity and resonates with the target audience.
